DMS climatology from Hulswar et al. (2022) https://doi.org/10.5194/essd-14-2963-2022 
Data available from https://doi.org/10.17632/hyn62spny2.1 or as netcdf from authors 

Rev3DMS_to_N96.ipynb regrids the Rev3 climatology for the N96 grid and appropriate UM land-sea mask. 
Billinear interpolation has been used for the regridding and linear interpolation accross longitudes for the resetting of the land sea mask.
